Our Mission

Our goal at the George Brown Tech Film Festival is to celebrate cinematic innovation while bridging the gap between technology and storytelling. We are dedicated to building a vibrant, welcoming community that encourages tech enthusiasts and supports emerging filmmakers.

Our main objective is to give students accessible opportunity to investigate the nexus between film and technology. To do this, we are happy to provide free admission and immersive experiences to students from various backgrounds, enabling them to interact with state-of-the-art cinematic technology and establish connections with professionals in the field.
